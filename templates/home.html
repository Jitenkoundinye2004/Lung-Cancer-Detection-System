What is Lung Cancer?

Lung cancer begins in the lungs and is one of the leading causes of cancer-related deaths worldwide. Early detection significantly improves the chances of successful treatment. Our system leverages deep learning models to analyze chest scans and identify common types of lung cancer such as Adenocarcinoma, Squamous Cell Carcinoma, and Large Cell Carcinoma.
